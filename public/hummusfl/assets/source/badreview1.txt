I was excited to see this food truck so close to my home, it's only five minutes away and I was really hoping to like it. 

It's a food truck that's there permanently and the area it's in is decorated really nicely with tables and a couple of comfy looking chairs. It also was very clean.  The staff that I met were super nice. 

I only ordered hummus to go, and since that's the name of the place, you would think that it would be absolutely delicious. Unfortunately I didn't find that to be the case. Sometimes the simplest dishes should  be the best dish if that makes sense. They do give you a generous portion but there was so much lemon in it that after a few bites I couldn't eat anymore. The consistency was perfect, nice and creamy but like I said the lemon they used was too overpowering. The pita bread they served with it was Just bad. It was so thin(see picture) and definitely not freshly made. I've had better pita from the grocery store.  

Won't be back since there are so many other Mediterranean restaurants in the area.
