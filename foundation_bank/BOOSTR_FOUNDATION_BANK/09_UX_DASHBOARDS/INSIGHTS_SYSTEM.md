# BOOSTR Insights System

## Working name

The name is not final.

"BOOSTR Pulse" was suggested but not approved. The user said it did not fully hit.

The concept is approved: a weekly or periodic insight system that gives each partner custom business feedback.

## Purpose

BOOSTR Insights is like a coach/check-up for a business.

It should help the partner understand:

- What is working.
- What is not working.
- What improved.
- What could generate money.
- What action to take next.

## Tone

Should not feel childish.
Should not feel corporate if partner is artist/creator.
Should feel motivating, grounded, and specific.

## Possible naming directions

- BOOSTR Check-In.
- BOOSTR Signal.
- BOOSTR Drop.
- BOOSTR Weekly Boost.
- BOOSTR Business Health.
- BOOSTR Progress.
- BOOSTR Status.

Name remains open.

## Frequency

Depends on partner type:

- Pilot/basic: weekly or every 10 days when useful.
- Monthly partner: weekly or planned recurring.
- Premium: weekly and more strategic.

## Insight categories

- Wins.
- Money generated.
- Audience signals.
- Leads.
- Sales.
- Content opportunities.
- Weak points.
- Next actions.
- Recommendations.

## Important philosophy

BOOSTR should not inflate vanity metrics like social media often does. It should show real progress and monetization, while still motivating the partner.
