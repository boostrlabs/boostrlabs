# Dashboard / Workspace Philosophy

## Core idea

BOOSTR should not build one generic dashboard for all partners.

Every partner should have a workspace that feels built for their business.

However, the backend/core data can be shared.

## Two primary workspaces

### BOOSTR Manager Workspace

Used by BOOSTR internal operators.

This is the first dashboard to build.

It should manage:

- BOOSTR leads.
- Partner approvals.
- Partner profiles.
- Modules.
- Smart Links.
- Partner inventory.
- Notes.
- Contracts status.
- Payments placeholders.
- Rewards/Balance placeholders.
- Insights.
- Tasks.

### Partner Workspace

Used by each partner.

It should show only what matters to that partner.

## Custom dashboards

Gemese dashboard should not be the same as Frank Barber, JANKO, Rouvssen, 82NGEL, SOLVE, Prieto, or Pedro Oberto Autosales.

Each partner should see:

- Relevant stats.
- Relevant language.
- Relevant colors.
- Relevant modules.
- Relevant wins.
- Relevant issues.

## Performance insights

The user wants performance cards that motivate partners without feeding fake ego.

BOOSTR should show:

- What is working.
- What improved since joining BOOSTR.
- What needs work.
- What can be monetized.
- What actions to take next.

## Example artist stats

- Total streams across platforms in last 7 days.
- Sales from beats/services.
- Apparel sold.
- Most clicked link.
- Best-performing content.
- New leads/fans.
- Revenue generated through BOOSTR.
- Next recommended action.

## Example local service stats

- Appointments booked.
- No-shows.
- Deposits collected.
- Services sold.
- Returning clients.
- Reviews.
- Top service.
- Next availability.

## Language adaptation

Artists may see:

- Career growth.
- Performance stats.
- Fans.
- Sales.
- Drops.
- Bookings.

Car sellers may see:

- Leads.
- Deals.
- Inventory.
- Approvals.
- Follow-ups.
- Closings.

Beauty/barbers may see:

- Agenda.
- Bookings.
- Services.
- Clients.
- Reviews.
- Capacity.

## Visual style

- Minimal.
- Apple-coded.
- Black/white.
- Dark red/wine accent.
- Floating cards.
- Clean type.
- No clutter.
- Each card must explain why it exists.
