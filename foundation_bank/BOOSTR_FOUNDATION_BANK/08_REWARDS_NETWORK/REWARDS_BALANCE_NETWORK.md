# BOOSTR Rewards, Balance, and Partner Network

## Core distinction

BOOSTR Balance and BOOSTR Rewards must be separate in v1.

## BOOSTR Balance

BOOSTR Balance represents money or value inside the platform that belongs to a partner or is owed according to rules.

It may eventually include:

- Revenue share payouts.
- Referral commissions.
- Partner earnings.
- Internal credits.
- Pending balances.

Cashout rules must be legally reviewed.

## BOOSTR Rewards

BOOSTR Rewards are gamified credits/benefits for partner actions.

Rewards are not crypto.
Rewards are not public currency.
Rewards are not freely transferable.
Rewards do not automatically create a debt obligation for BOOSTR.
Rewards cannot be used to force partners to provide services unless the accepting partner agrees.

## How partners may earn rewards

- Referring new partners.
- Running first campaign.
- Completing onboarding.
- Hitting performance milestones.
- Providing testimonial/case study.
- Offering discounts to BOOSTR network.
- Participating in partner promotions.

## How rewards may be used

Possible future uses:

- Discounts on BOOSTR services.
- Campaign credits.
- Partner marketplace discounts.
- Upgrades.
- Tools.
- Services from partners who accept rewards.

## What rewards cannot do in v1

- No crypto.
- No public trading.
- No automatic cashout.
- No free conversion to USD without rules.
- No unlimited liability for BOOSTR.
- No forced acceptance by partners.

## Partner Network

BOOSTR Partner Network is a future ecosystem where partners can benefit from each other.

Examples:

- Photographer offers discount to BOOSTR partners.
- Producer offers discounted beats/services.
- Barber offers partner discount.
- SOLVE/car seller helps another partner buy a car.

## Car down payment concept

A powerful future marketing/use-case:

An artist or barber earns BOOSTR Rewards through activity/referrals/sales. Later, they need a car. They access BOOSTR Partner Marketplace and use accumulated rewards/benefits toward a car-related offer through SOLVE or a car seller partner. SOLVE gets a closed deal, the partner gets a better deal, and BOOSTR demonstrates ecosystem value.

This should be designed carefully:

- Rewards should not falsely represent guaranteed cash.
- Car partner must accept the reward/discount terms.
- BOOSTR should avoid becoming lender or financial institution.
- Any down payment support must be structured legally and transparently.

## Referral program v0.1

Default idea:

- Rewards by default.
- Cash only if referred partner pays cash.
- Recurring commissions only by special agreement.
- Referral terms must define eligibility, payout timing, abuse prevention, and caps.
