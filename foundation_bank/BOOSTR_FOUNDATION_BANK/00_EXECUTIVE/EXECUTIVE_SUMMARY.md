# BOOSTR Labs LLC — Executive Summary

BOOSTR Labs LLC is a Technology Solutions and Business Infrastructure Services Provider.

BOOSTR creates custom digital infrastructure for creators, artists, founders, small businesses, local service providers, beauty businesses, automotive operators, and other creative businesses that have something to sell but need a better system to sell it.

BOOSTR is not a website agency. A website is only one possible interface. BOOSTR is not a CRM, not Linktree, not Wix, not Booksy, not a label, not a distribution company, not an artist manager, and not a talent agency.

BOOSTR studies each partner and builds a custom sales/growth system around what they sell, how they sell, who they sell to, what they already have, and how much operational structure they need.

The company has a boutique, high-attention model. It does not need thousands of customers. It can grow with 50-100 strong partners, different revenue models, and highly optimized remote workflows.

## Key thesis

Every business has something to sell. BOOSTR turns that into a system.

## Core products

- BOOSTR Smart Link: entry product and acquisition tool.
- BOOSTR Business OS: deeper infrastructure for qualified partners.
- BOOSTR Partner Workspace: dashboard/custom workspace for each partner.
- BOOSTR Manager Workspace: internal dashboard for BOOSTR operators.
- BOOSTR Inventory: products, services, packages, bookings, beats, apparel, offers, custom quotes.
- BOOSTR Deep Links: direct links for every offer/detail/campaign context.
- BOOSTR Payments: smart payment link and future payment routing layer.
- BOOSTR Rewards and Balance: separate internal systems for rewards, credits, commissions, and/or platform balances.
- BOOSTR Partner Network: future marketplace/network of partner benefits, referrals, and cross-business value.
- BOOSTR Insights: custom weekly or periodic performance reports/cards for each partner.

## Economic model

BOOSTR earns through modules and agreements, not one fixed pricing model.

Valid revenue streams:

- Startup fee.
- Setup/project fee.
- Monthly infrastructure/maintenance fee.
- Revenue share for defined modules/offers/campaigns.
- Payment processing / smart payment link fees.
- Campaign setup/management fees.
- Custom work fees.
- White-label / buyout / export / license fees.
- Referral/reward-linked growth incentives.

Revenue share does not automatically apply to all of a partner's business. It applies only to the defined modules/products/services/campaigns/payment flows in the signed agreement.
