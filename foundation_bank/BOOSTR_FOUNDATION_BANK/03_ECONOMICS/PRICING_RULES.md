# Pricing Rules v0.1

These are not final prices. They are operating ranges for early BOOSTR.

## Entry / pilot

- Startup fee: $30-$50.
- Optional minimum monthly fee: $19-$49.
- Revenue share if BOOSTR generates/processes sales.
- Powered by BOOSTR required.
- Performance data usage required.

## Smart Link

Smart Link can be free for selected partners as a marketing/acquisition strategy.

Free Smart Link does not mean unlimited work.

Free or subsidized Smart Link should require:

- Powered by BOOSTR.
- Permission to use as case study/portfolio if successful.
- Agreement to revenue share on defined BOOSTR-generated revenue if applicable.
- Limits on revisions/support.

## Business OS

Business OS is not free by default.

Possible models:

- Setup + monthly.
- Startup fee + revenue share.
- Monthly only.
- Custom project fee.
- Internal/pilot venture case.

## Custom work

Anything that does not already exist, or requires strategy/design from scratch, is custom quoted.

This includes:

- Full custom dashboard.
- Advanced automation.
- Custom app logic.
- Integration with third parties.
- Complex campaigns.
- Full brand direction.
- White-label/export.
- Non-standard support.

## Monthly support bands

Early rough ranges:

- Basic: $19-$49/month.
- Small business support: $49-$99/month.
- Growth support: $99-$299/month.
- Premium/custom: $299+/month.

These ranges can change.
