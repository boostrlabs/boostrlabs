# Company Identity

## Official name

BOOSTR Labs LLC

## Legal positioning

BOOSTR Labs LLC is a Technology Solutions and Business Infrastructure Services Provider.

## Plain-language positioning

BOOSTR is a lab of digital builders that helps businesses, creators, artists, and founders boost their business, career, digital presence, leads, sales, and money-making ability.

## What BOOSTR does

BOOSTR studies what a partner already has and builds the custom digital infrastructure needed to help that partner sell better.

BOOSTR can create:

- Smart Links.
- Landing pages.
- Full websites.
- Business OS dashboards.
- Partner workspaces.
- Product/service inventories.
- Lead capture systems.
- Payment links.
- Deep Links.
- Campaign workflows.
- Performance insights.
- Custom assets and branding.

## What BOOSTR is not

BOOSTR is not:

- A record label.
- A music distributor.
- An artist manager.
- A road manager.
- A talent agency.
- A W2 employer.
- A default 1099 contractor relationship.
- A generic CRM.
- Linktree.
- Wix.
- Booksy.
- A website-only company.
- A self-service builder.

## Company philosophy

BOOSTR does not force a business into a generic system. BOOSTR builds the system around the business.

The Smart Link is the easiest entry point, but the goal is larger: infrastructure.

BOOSTR combines business taste, creative direction, technology, and operational structure. AI can accelerate execution, but human taste, consistency, and strategy lead the process.

## Long-term founder objective

BOOSTR Labs and SOLVE should eventually run through trained people, documented systems, and automation, allowing JANKO DIORR and 82NGEL to focus on music, creativity, and strategic monitoring from home.
