## HUMAN DECISIONS

- Smart Link is the entry product for BOOSTR.
- Smart Links are manually designed after studying the partner business.
- BOOSTR should be selective; not everyone can open an account.
- BOOSTR Manager creates accounts.
- SOLVE remains external and live on its own domain/hosting.
- SOLVE can be used as technical reference and case study.
- Do not use "managed by" because it implies artist management.
- BOOSTR is not a label, not a manager, and does not sign artists.
- Revenue share should be flexible and module-specific.
- Pilots may start with a low startup fee of about $30-$50.
- Minimum monthly infrastructure fee can be $19-$49 where applicable.
- Pilot partners must accept revenue share, Powered by BOOSTR, and use of performance data.
- Powered by BOOSTR should remain unless removed through buyout, white-label, or special contract.
- Rewards and Balance should be separate in v1.
- First dashboard should be BOOSTR Manager.
- First partner is 82NGEL/Johanka.
- Leads must be separated into BOOSTR leads and Partner leads.
- Partner Inventory means each partner's products, services, offers, bookings, packages, etc.
- PRIME technical standard is required.
