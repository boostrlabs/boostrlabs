# Smart Links and Deep Links

## BOOSTR Smart Link

The BOOSTR Smart Link is the entry product.

It is designed to help a person/business convert attention into action.

It is not a generic link list. It is a custom conversion interface based on what the partner sells and what the audience needs to do next.

## Manual by design

Smart Links are not automatically generated in v1.

BOOSTR studies the partner and creates the Smart Link manually.

Reason:

- Higher quality.
- Better strategy.
- More premium perception.
- Better customization.
- Fewer generic templates.

## Every partner should have a Smart Link

Even if a partner has a full website, BOOSTR Smart Link can act as:

- Fast landing point.
- Social bio link.
- Campaign link hub.
- Direct conversion layer.
- Portal to website or offers.

## Deep Links

Deep Links are specific direct links to individual products, services, offers, songs, beats, outfits, bookings, campaigns, or pages.

Examples:

- Story with outfit -> apparel link.
- Story with new song -> listen link.
- Story making beat -> buy custom beat link.
- Story with specific beat -> direct beat purchase link.
- Beauty post -> exact appointment service.
- Barber post -> exact booking/service.
- Car post -> exact vehicle/lead form.

## Why Deep Links matter

Many products are already emotionally sold through content. The buying path should be two clicks.

Generic link pages add friction.
Deep Links reduce friction.

## Deep Link fields

A Deep Link should eventually support:

- Partner.
- Offer/inventory item.
- Title.
- Description.
- Media.
- CTA.
- Price.
- Availability.
- Source tracking.
- Campaign tracking.
- Payment/booking/contact action.
