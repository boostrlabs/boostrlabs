# BOOSTR Products Overview

BOOSTR products are modular. A partner can enter with a Smart Link and later activate deeper infrastructure.

## Product categories

1. BOOSTR Smart Link.
2. BOOSTR Business OS.
3. BOOSTR Partner Workspace.
4. BOOSTR Manager Workspace.
5. BOOSTR Inventory.
6. BOOSTR Deep Links.
7. BOOSTR Payments.
8. BOOSTR Insights.
9. BOOSTR Campaigns.
10. BOOSTR Rewards / Balance.
11. BOOSTR Partner Network.
12. Custom websites/web apps.
13. Branding/assets.
14. Custom OS builds.

## Smart Link

Entry product.

Must be custom-designed manually per partner.

Not a Linktree clone.

Not automatically generated.

Can be free/subsidized for selected partners.

## Business OS

A larger system for partners who need more:

- Dashboard.
- Leads.
- Inventory.
- Payments.
- Insights.
- Campaigns.
- Workflows.
- Documents/files.
- Partner-specific metrics.

Rule:

Everyone can have a Smart Link. Not everyone qualifies for Business OS.

## Custom websites and apps

BOOSTR can still sell websites, web apps, and Custom OS as one-time or recurring projects. The existence of the broader ecosystem does not remove the ability to sell front-facing services.

## Products should not be oversold

A partner may only need a Smart Link.
Another may need full Business OS.
Another may only need branding.
BOOSTR chooses based on analysis, not a generic sales script.
