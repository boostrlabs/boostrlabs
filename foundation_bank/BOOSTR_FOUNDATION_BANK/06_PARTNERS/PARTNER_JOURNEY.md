# Partner Journey

## Overview

BOOSTR Partner Journey defines how a lead becomes a partner.

## Step 1: Lead enters

Sources:

- Instagram.
- TikTok.
- Referral.
- Website.
- WhatsApp.
- Networking.
- Existing friends/network.
- Portfolio/case studies.
- Paid leads later.

## Step 2: Soft Pull / Pre-qualification

The partner answers a short form or has a conversation.

BOOSTR checks:

- What they sell.
- Whether they are serious.
- Whether BOOSTR can help.
- Current revenue/structure.
- Audience/reach.
- Risk.
- Support needs.
- Willingness to collaborate.

## Step 3: Decision

Possible outcomes:

- Accepted.
- Waitlist.
- Rejected for now.
- Needs more info.
- Offer only one-time service.
- Offer Smart Link pilot.
- Offer Business OS.

If rejected, BOOSTR should send a professional explanation and invite them to reapply later when realistic conditions change.

## Step 4: Hard Pull / Deep Business Analysis

For approved partners, BOOSTR studies:

- Business model.
- Products/services.
- Audience.
- Branding.
- Current tools.
- Current flow.
- Sales process.
- Pain points.
- Revenue potential.
- Risk.
- Best entry product.
- Best agreement model.

## Step 5: Proposal

BOOSTR proposes:

- Modules.
- Economics.
- Legal agreement.
- Startup fee or setup.
- Monthly/revenue share.
- Support frequency.
- Powered by BOOSTR terms.

## Step 6: Account creation

BOOSTR Manager creates:

- Partner Profile.
- Partner Workspace.
- Smart Link.
- Inventory records.
- Lead capture.
- Relevant placeholders.

## Step 7: Launch

Partner receives the first public interface:

- Smart Link.
- Deep Links if applicable.
- Initial dashboard/cards.
- Instructions.

## Step 8: Monitoring

BOOSTR monitors performance:

- Wins.
- Weaknesses.
- Leads.
- Sales.
- Audience actions.
- Content opportunities.
- What to improve.

## Step 9: Growth meetings

Frequency depends on plan:

- Pilot/basic: weekly or every 10 days if needed.
- Monthly: recurring according to plan.
- Premium: weekly.

## Step 10: Upgrade / switch model

Partner can evolve:

- Smart Link -> Business OS.
- Revenue share -> monthly fee.
- Basic support -> premium.
- Manual payments -> smart payment link.
- Single offer -> full inventory.
