# 08 — BOOSTR for Artists

## Status

Confirmed module/sub-brand.

## Definition

BOOSTR for Artists empowers artists with technology, data, business infrastructure, and optional strategic/creative advisory without imposing creative decisions.

## Core rule

La data recomienda. El artista decide.

## What BOOSTR for Artists does

- Artist OS;
- Smart Links;
- Fan CRM;
- presaves;
- merch/drops;
- BNPL;
- tickets/events;
- sponsor pages;
- heat maps;
- Demand Radar;
- Fan Passport;
- insights;
- follow-up;
- creative advisory if requested or plan includes it;
- rollout advisory;
- aesthetic/visual guidance if requested.

## What BOOSTR for Artists does not do

- does not control creativity;
- does not impose sound;
- does not impose image;
- does not act as a disquera;
- does not take masters by default;
- does not act as mandatory manager;
- does not sell generic creative services as BOOSTR agency work.

## Tiers conceptually

### 1. Artist OS Only
Artist already has concept, sound, clothing, aesthetics, and rollout. BOOSTR handles business infrastructure.

### 2. Artist OS + Advisory
BOOSTR advises on rollout, CTAs, data, campaign timing, product movement, content ideas, and business decisions.

### 3. Artist OS + Creative Direction
BOOSTR helps with aesthetics, visual direction, clothing/drop, campaign, content, cover, narrative, and rollout when the artist wants it.

## Positioning phrase

BOOSTR for Artists gives artists the business layer of a major label without giving away creative control.

Spanish internal version:

BOOSTR for Artists te da el business layer de una disquera, sin entregar tu control creativo.
