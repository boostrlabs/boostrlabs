# 09 — BOOSTR Network

## Status

Confirmed concept.

## Definition

BOOSTR Network is a curated network of partners, services, and opportunities inside the BOOSTR ecosystem.

It is not an open marketplace.

## What it is not

- Not Fiverr.
- Not a generic marketplace.
- Not a public catalog where anyone sells anything.
- Not BOOSTR selling agency services.

## What it is

A curated internal discovery/recommendation layer where partners can be connected to other trusted partners or services when it makes sense.

Examples:

- artist looking for production;
- producer selling beats/licenses;
- artist looking for visuals;
- creator looking for apparel/drop support;
- partner looking for photographer/videographer;
- collab opportunities between compatible artists.

## Case: Janko/WESTDETRO beat store

Janko Diorr / WESTDETRO can sell beats and music services through infrastructure powered by BOOSTR:

- beat previews;
- license tiers;
- contracts/PDF;
- digital signature;
- payments;
- BNPL;
- delivery;
- customer/order tracking;
- post-purchase page;
- follow-up.

The service remains Janko/WESTDETRO's creative service. BOOSTR powers the infrastructure and may collect fees/revenue share according to agreement.

## UX rule

Do not say “BOOSTR sells beats.”

Say:

- “Janko Diorr Beat Store powered by BOOSTR.”
- “WESTDETRO services powered by BOOSTR.”
- “Curated through BOOSTR Network.”

## Discovery inside dashboard

Cards should be contextual:

- “Looking for production?”
- “Need visuals?”
- “A compatible collab is available.”
- “This sound is moving. Ask BOOSTR Advisor for production options.”
