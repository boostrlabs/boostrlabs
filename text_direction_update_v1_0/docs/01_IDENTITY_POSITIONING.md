# 01 — Identity and Positioning

## Core identity

BOOSTR Labs LLC is a parent technology company that builds custom business operating systems and intelligence layers for partners.

## What BOOSTR is

- Technology infrastructure.
- Business OS / Custom OS builder.
- Data and intelligence layer.
- Partner operating system.
- Growth and conversion infrastructure.
- Modular platform activated by need, plan, agreement, role, and vertical.

## What BOOSTR is not

- Not an agency.
- Not a generic web design shop.
- Not a disquera.
- Not a manager by default.
- Not an open marketplace.
- Not a generic CRM.
- Not Linktree.
- Not Booksy.
- Not a generic AI company.
- Not a self-service builder.

## Core philosophy

Cada negocio merece su propio sistema.

BOOSTR gives small/independent partners access to tools that usually make large brands look more powerful: checkout, BNPL, data, follow-up, dashboards, rewards, fan/customer memory, tickets, sponsor pages, insights, and automated operations.

## Human language

BOOSTR must sound human, not AI. UI and copy should be clear, minimal, and specific.

Bad direction:

- corporate filler;
- generic AI paragraphs;
- internal notes shown to partners;
- unnecessary technical language;
- too many cards/data points in artist dashboards.

Good direction:

- direct;
- visual;
- useful;
- partner-specific;
- short;
- action-oriented;
- written in the language the partner understands.

## Brand feel

BOOSTR should feel premium, futuristic, simple, product-like, Apple/Tesla-coded where appropriate, black/premium/CMD-console inspired where appropriate, but always adapted to the partner's world.

Partner examples:

- 82NGEL: red/black/y2k, visual, minimal text, app-like, mobile-first.
- GEMESE: black/platinum/urban, sober, not red, no corporate AI copy.
- Janko/WESTDETRO: premium, black, underground, Apple/Spotify-coded, music-tech.
